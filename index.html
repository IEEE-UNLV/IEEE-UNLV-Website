About Us

IEEE at UNLV is dedicated to the education and professional development of students in the field of electrical, electronics, communications, computer engineering, and computer science. We strive for the enhancing the learning experience through viable means.

Get Involved

We provide opportunties to participate in tech conferences, workshops, and volunteering opportunities to stay connected with our community. These allow our memebers to make professional connections and gain valuable experience to help start a career.

Become a Member

Anyone UNLV student can join us no matter your background. Come with an open mind and ready to learn!
