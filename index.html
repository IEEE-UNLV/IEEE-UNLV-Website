About Us

IEEE at UNLV is dedicated to the education and professional development of students in the fields of electrical, electronics, communications, computer engineering, and computer science. We strive to enhance the learning experience through viable means.

Get Involved

We offer opportunities to participate in tech conferences, workshops, and volunteer activities, allowing you to stay connected with our community. These opportunities enable our members to establish professional connections and gain valuable experience to help launch their careers.
